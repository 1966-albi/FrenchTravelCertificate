Easily create your travel certificates for France during the quarantine. This app provide a QR Code
that can be used by a police officer to attest that your "travel" has a valid reason.

With this app you can save your profile (no data collected) in order to auto-fill your certificate
when you need it.

-Easy to use
-Fast
-Open-source
-No data collection
-Ad-free

The app has the "Tracking" AntiFeature because it auto-sends data linked to crashes using ACRA to a
Tracepot server. To know more about this implementation, feel free to go to
https://github.com/PeredurOmega/FrenchTravelCertificate/blob/f-droid/app/src/main/java/com/pi/attestation/MyApplication.kt
